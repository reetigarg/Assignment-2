About Us

Econtago is the official Economics Association of CHRIST (Deemed to be University), Bannerghatta Road Campus. We strive to foster economic awareness, critical thinking, and academic engagement through flagship events, discussions, and competitions.

Events We Host

Arthotsav

Arthotsav is an economics-focused event designed to blend analytical thinking, creativity, and competitive spirit through curated activities and challenges.

1776 Syndrome

Our flagship national-level economics fest featuring debates, quizzes, simulations, policy discussions, and expert panels.

Vrittee

Vrittee focuses on intellectual discourse, student-led discussions, and platforms that encourage research-based economic conversations.

Many More

In addition to our flagship events, Econtago hosts workshops, panel discussions, budget analyses, and collaborative academic initiatives throughout the year.
